Export Pylon Projects

↑ Drag this button to your Bookmarks Bar (top of browser)

1
Show Bookmarks Bar: Press Ctrl + Shift + B (Windows) or Cmd + Shift + B (Mac).
2
Install: Drag the green "Export Pylon Projects" button up into your Bookmarks Bar.
3
Use: Open Pylon, go to your project list, and click the bookmark.
